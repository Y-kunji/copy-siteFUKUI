Map & Access

Illust map

By car

  • About 3.5 hours from Osaka
  • About 2.5 hours from Nagoya
    (You can use "Takefu IC" at Meishin Expressway to Hokuriku Expressway)
  • About 2 hours from Kanazawa
    (Use "Sabae IC" at Hokuriku Expressway)

By train / To nearest JR Takefu station

  • About 2 hours from Osaka
  • About 2 hours from Nagoya
  • About 1 hour from Kanazawa
  • About 3.5 hours from Tokyo
    * About 1 hour from "JR Takefu station" by Fukutetsu bus to "Ikeda-cho (Inari bus stop)"
